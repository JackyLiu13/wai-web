Our Mission

Western AI

Founded in 2018, WAI is a registered club at Western University and the with 750+ members. WAI is committed to developing future leaders in AI across all industries by connecting and educating students through unique projects, competitions and educational events.

Western AI Social!

Rec Room

Organized and hosted by WesternAI, the Rec Room social is one of a kind. Bringing together members from different factors to enjoy an amazing event as one. Keep on the look out for our next event!

Western AI Events!

Data Quest

DataQuest is a three-day hackathon on March 10-12, centered around data science and artificial intelligence. The event will be a hybrid format, starting with virtual workshops on data analytics and machine learning from March 6-9. The first and last days of the event will also be virtual (opening ceremonies, judging, and closing ceremonies), but on the second day we invite you to join us IN-PERSON at Western University to grab swag and food, network with company representatives, and attend workshops!

Western AI Events!

CUCAI

A conference of 300+ guests attracting top talent from across Canada. This year, they are hosting the conference at Queen's University, on March 4th and 5th. CUCAI brings together students looking to do more than their classwork. The community is one where we actively challenge the status quo around what undergraduate students are capable of achieving in the field of artificial intelligence. Makers, innovators and disruptors alike.

Western AI Initiatives!

If you have not yet, be sure to subscribe to Wavelength for cool exclusive info about internships, artificial intelligence, and tech!
